What we do?

SolRx is consulting International Pharmacists and Pharmacy Technicians who are planning to come to Canada and get licensed to practice as pharmacists and pharmacy technicians in Canada. Guiding and helping them in regard to the administrative paperwork involved to get licensed and to further help them refresh their past qualifications to prepare them for the Canadian healthcare job market.

This will involve honing their technical skills such as introducing them to the pharmacy computer software used in Canada and consulting them on the counseling and vocational skills needed for the Canadian job market. Furthermore, we consult prospective employers with healthcare recruitment services. workers.
